Our Story

Jessica and Matthew met during their sophomore year at the University of Kentucky during a meeting of their campus ministry CRU. Matthew immediately saw her in the crowd and knew that he had to meet her. They quickly became friends and spent the next year hanging out with each other until the beginning of junior year when Jessica finally caved and decided to go out with him. Immediately the love and connection between them grew and the rest is history...
